# RhombixTechnologies_Tasks
Collection of technical and graphic design tasks completed during my internship at Rhombix Technologies.
## 💻 Project: HP EliteBook Product Promotion
**Objective:** Create a high-conversion social media post focusing on brand consistency and visual hierarchy.

### 🎨 Design Features:
* **Focal Point:** Centered product placement with a custom technical wallpaper to enhance realism.
* **Typography:** Used high-contrast Sans-Serif fonts to establish a clear reading path from the discount to the CTA.
* **Color Theory:** Combined a premium cream/gold aesthetic with high-energy "Electric Cyan" for action-oriented elements.
* **Composition:** Applied the Rule of Thirds and negative space to ensure the design feels breathable yet impactful.

### 🛠️ Tools Used:
* Canva (Layout and Asset Management)
* Brand Design Principles (Hierarchy, Alignment, Contrast)
